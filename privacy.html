Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This page explains how the EPF Withdrawal & Maturity Register website ("this site," "we," "us") handles information when you use it. This site is an independent estimation tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) or the Government of India.

Information you enter into the calculator

All figures you type into the withdrawal or maturity calculator — such as your PF balance, salary, or years of service — are processed entirely within your own browser. This data is never transmitted to, or stored on, our servers. Closing or refreshing the page clears it completely.

Automatically collected information

Like most websites, this site may use third-party services (such as Google AdSense and standard web hosting logs) that automatically collect limited technical information, including your approximate location (based on IP address), browser type, device type, and pages visited. We do not control this collection directly; it is governed by the relevant third party's own privacy policy, linked below.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses this data, and manage your ad personalization settings, at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

No accounts, no personal data storage

This site does not require account creation, does not collect names, email addresses, or financial account details, and does not sell or share any personal information, because none is collected or stored by us in the first place.

Accuracy disclaimer

Figures produced by this calculator are estimates for general planning purposes only, based on publicly available rules that are subject to change. This site does not provide financial, tax, or legal advice. Always verify current rates and rules directly with EPFO or a qualified professional before making financial decisions.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the tools or services used on this site. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this policy, you can reach us via the contact details listed on the main site.
